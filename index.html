Country Information

Nepal, officially known as Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal is landlocked country located between India and Republic of China. Nepal is home of himalayas. Nepal has 8 out of top 14 highest mountains of the world including the roof of the world Mt. Everest. Mountains are not the only attraction of Nepal but other natural scenery, cultural experince, advantural activities and beautiful garden of ancient architectures are the main attraction of Nepal. Natural Scenary

Nepal is only one percent of the total land area of the world but it is fully covered by beautiful natural scenarys.About 68 percent of the total land is covered by mountains and thirty two percent of the land is plain area known as tarai. Hilly regions is devided in to two parts as himalayas and mountains.Nepal has varied landcape from as low as 60 meters from the sea level to the highest point on earth as 8848 meters within the width of 193 kilometeres(119 miles).About one third of the total area is protected as national parks. Beautiful mountains with forest and the waterfalls from the tall mountains will give you the feeling that you would just want to stay there to watch them forever.

Cultural Experince

Nepal is not only rich in natural resoces it is also rich in culture. Nepal is melting pot of hinduism and buddism. About 81 percent of the the total population is hindu about 10 percent of the population practes buddism. Nepal has as many as gods as people (32 million), as many festivals as days in a year. Within this small portion of the world Nepal has about 93 ethnic groups and 120 spoken languages and dialects.When you visit Nepal it is very important to know some of the cultural values such as greeting as namaste (putting your two palms togather), handshakes are common as well, eating food or passing something with left hand considered as inapproperiate,you are expected to take your shoes off while entering the religious temples.

Adventure

While you are in Nepal do not forget to take your adventure experince to next level by climbing the mountains to be on the roof of the world leaving your footprints on their golden history. You can go on trecking the mountains, rock climbing on the mountains, rafting , jungle safari, bungee jumping. If you are in Nepal at right time you can participate in elephant polo world cup as well.

Architectures

Nepal is not only the home of natural architecture but also the home of ancient human architectures as well as mordern architectures. Nepal has the architecture that was built around 300BC. Most of the old architectures have many arts on the building walls and artistic sturctures.

Hospitality

while you are in Nepal you will also experince good hospitality. Most of the people understand english langueage in case if you get lost on the way back to your motel. Different local and international food will give you the taste of the world.
